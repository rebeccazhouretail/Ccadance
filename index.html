CV Builder — How it works

From draft notes to qualified CV in 7 steps

1

Choose your path

New to luxury retail or experienced SA? Different questions, different CV structure — built for where you are now.

2

Enter your profile

Name, location, languages. The system knows that speaking Mandarin + Cantonese + English is a top-3 skill for luxury retail.

3

Tell your story

3 guided questions. The system writes your personal statement — no vague buzzwords, no "passionate about fashion".

4

Add your experience

Up to 3 roles. The system enforces the rule: at least one achievement with a number per role. Earlier roles condensed automatically.

5

Capacity — scored automatically

14 questions mapped to a 20-skill library. The system scores every skill and shows only your top 3 — the ones with the strongest evidence.

6

Career expectation

Four lines: target role, target environment, skills to develop, salary expectation. Clear and direct — no heavy paragraph.

7

References & download

Two references for background check. Preview your CV, toggle EN / CN, download as PDF. Done.
